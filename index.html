Potential Causes

  • Increased work stress
  • Lack of quality sleep
  • Reduced physical activity

Suggested Solutions

  1. Practice stress-reduction techniques like deep breathing for 5 minutes when you feel overwhelmed
  2. Establish a consistent sleep schedule aiming for 7-9 hours nightly
  3. Incorporate short walks into your daily routine, even 10 minutes can help
  4. Consider keeping a mood journal to identify specific triggers
